What we're working on next.

These features are planned for future updates, in no particular order.

  • Medical bed (adult and child), bed sheets, and pillows.
  • Medical hand sanitizer, gloves, and more medication.
  • Medical curtains, wheelchairs, and walking equipment.
  • Medical operation tools and general supplies.
  • Bandages, gauze, medical tape, and thermometers.
  • Blood pressure monitors, sterile saline solution, and surgical masks.
  • Disinfectant sprays and wipes, syringes, needles, and medical scissors.
  • Tongue depressors and stethoscopes.
  • Pain relievers (Ibuprofen, aspirin), antacids, and antihistamines.
  • Cough drops, cough syrup, anti-diarrhea medication, and cold/flu remedies.
  • Topical antibiotic ointments and wound care creams.
